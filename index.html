Let's pay tribute to the life of

John F. Kennedy

November 22, 1963 | Parkland Hospital

_________

John Fitzgerald Kennedy was an American politician who served as the 35th president of the United States from 1961 until his assassination, which occurred near the end of his third year in office. Despite the many oppositions he received during his term, he continued to faithfully abide and serve the country in the best interest. His death was a testimony of that.
